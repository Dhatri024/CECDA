# CECDA Pilot Annotation Guidelines

## Claim Identification
A claim is included only if it contains:
- "We propose"
- "We introduce"
- "We show"
- "We outperform"
- "Our experiments demonstrate"

Exclude:
- Background statements
- Related work summaries
- Pure motivation sentences

## CSV Scoring
LOW: cautious / narrow claim  
MEDIUM: clear claim but limited scope  
HIGH: strong comparative or generalization claim  

## EBV Scoring
LOW: 1 dataset, no baselines  
MEDIUM: some experiments, limited breadth  
HIGH: multiple datasets + comparisons + ablations
